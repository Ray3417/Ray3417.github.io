Long-range Diffusion for Weakly Camouflaged Object Segmentation

Neural Networks, vol. 192, pp. 107915, 2025

Rui Wang, Caijuan Shi, Weixiang Gao, Changyu Duan, Ao Cai, Fei Yu, Yunchao Wei

abstract

Weakly supervised camouflaged object segmentation (WSCOS) aims to segment objects well embedded in surroundings via the supervision of sparse annotations. This paper proposes the long-range diffusion network (LRDNet) to diffuse the sparse annotations for improving WSCOS performance. A novel gated local saliency coherence (GLSC) loss is designed to efficiently diffuse limited annotation information across the entire image. Additionally, the Trans-decorator and the restoration upsampling (RUp) are designed to capture sufficient long-range dependencies.

Camouflaged Object Segmentation with Prior via Two-stage Training

Computer Vision and Image Understanding, vol. 246, pp. 104061, 2024

Rui Wang, Caijuan Shi, Changyu Duan, Weixiang Gao, Hongli Zhu, Yunchao Wei, Meiqin Liu

abstract

The camouflaged object segmentation (COS) task aims to segment objects visually embedded within the background. This paper proposes a prior-guided transformer (PGT) for prior generation and guidance. A prior generation subnetwork consisting of a Transformer backbone and simple convolutions obtains higher-quality priors. A novel two-stage training method achieves the backbone's deep supervision. The prior guidance modules (PGM) with distinct space token mixers capture global dependencies of location priors and local details of boundary priors.
